This app makes it possible to add comments to your flows.
The cards contain just text
The cards are not active in any way

Use it to comment your flows. Add explanations on why you add a card. Add explanations on what a card does

examples:

* This condition was added to prevent running the flow when I am not at home
* This math.js calculation will round the value to 2 digits. Chromecast cannot deal with multiple digits after the comma.
